Dynamic Accessible Graphs Demonstration

This is a demonstration of integrating accessibility into graphs on a website. It is intended to show the concept, and many updates will have to be made to make the idea ready for production.

Data is being dynamically loaded from a CSV file. The audio and text descriptions do not change, but could be implemented now with a hook-in with the accessible graphing engine.
